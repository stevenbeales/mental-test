    StandardError

    class Thor::Error

    Thor::Error is raised when it's caused by wrong usage of thor classes. Those errors have their backtrace suppressed and are nicely shown to the user.

    Errors that are caused by the developer, like declaring a method which overwrites a thor keyword, SHOULD NOT raise a Thor::Error. This way, we ensure that developer errors are shown with full backtrace.
